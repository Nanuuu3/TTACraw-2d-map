This site shows a Minecraft top-down map as fast, zoomable tiles. To add your map, turn your rendered PNG into tiles first:

  1. Open the tiler.
  2. Drop your rendered map_*.png and click Generate.
  3. Unzip the result here, next to index.html (you should get a tiles/ folder and a map.json), then reload.
